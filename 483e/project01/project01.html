DESIGN PHILOSOPHY.

My design philosophy is that it is necessary to follow the rules of design but also it needs to be broken at times. I believe that if everyone follows the exact rules and formulas of design, all design will be the same and the creativity and spark will disappear. When you break the rules of design you will innovate and create the new norm that people will follow. I also believe that it is important to stay on top of design trends because they are always changing and you want to be relevant when it comes to design.
